Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

Public URL: https://solder3t.github.io/pages/privacy.html

Applies to: Tunes Media Player (com.tunes.mp)

Tunes is primarily an offline music player. Your local music library, playback history, and app settings are stored on your device.

Data Access and Permissions

Tunes requests media permissions to read local audio files for playback: READ_MEDIA_AUDIO (Android 13+) and READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and below).

Optional Online Lyrics

If you enable online lyrics, the app may send limited track metadata (for example title and artist) to third-party lyrics providers over HTTPS to fetch lyrics.

Data Sharing

Tunes does not sell personal data. Metadata is transmitted only when needed for optional lyrics retrieval.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data remains on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the app. Tunes does not require account creation and does not maintain server-side user accounts.

Security

Network traffic for online requests uses HTTPS. The app does not intentionally expose user data to insecure cleartext transport.

Children's Privacy

Tunes is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law unless explicitly declared.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated as app features or legal requirements change. Updates will be published on this page with a revised date.
