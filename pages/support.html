Support

Last updated: April 5, 2026

This is the official support page for Musaic Player (com.musaic.mp), developed by Solder3t. If you encounter a bug, crash, or unexpected behavior, please reach out using the contact information below.

Contact

We aim to respond within 48 hours on business days. For faster resolution, please include the details listed below.

When Reporting a Bug

  • App version — visible in Settings → About Musaic Player
  • Device model (e.g., Pixel 8 Pro, Samsung Galaxy S24)
  • Android version (e.g., Android 15)
  • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • Screenshots or screen recordings if applicable

Feature Requests

Have an idea for a feature? Use the same email address and prefix your subject line with [Feature Request].

Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't Musaic find my music files?

Make sure you've granted the Audio permission during onboarding or in Settings → Permissions. If you selected a specific scan folder, ensure your music files are inside that folder.

How do I enable Online Lyrics?

Go to Settings → Behavior → Online Lyrics and toggle it on. This sends the current song's title, artist, and duration to lyric services. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I edit track metadata?

Long-press any track in your library, then select Edit Properties. Changes are written to the audio file's ID3 tags and will survive library rescans.
