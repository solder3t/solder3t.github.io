Frequently Asked Questions

Why can't Musaic find my music files?

Make sure you've granted the Audio permission during onboarding or in Settings → Permissions. If you selected a specific scan folder, ensure your music files are inside that folder.

How do I enable Online Lyrics?

Go to Settings → Behavior → Online Lyrics and toggle it on. This sends the current song's title, artist, and duration to lyric services. See our Privacy Policy for details.
