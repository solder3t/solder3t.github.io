Who We Are

About Solder3t

Solder3t is an independent Android development studio focused on building tools that respect your privacy and just work. We believe great software doesn't need to track you, show you ads, or force you to create an account.

Every app we ship is Kotlin-first, built with modern Android architecture, and designed to feel native and fluid. We obsess over the details — from smooth animations to offline-first functionality — because the little things are what make the difference.

Privacy First

No accounts, no trackers, no analytics. Your data stays on your device.

Performance

Buttery smooth 60fps, instant startup, and offline-first architecture.

Modern Stack

Jetpack Compose, Coroutines, Room, Media3 — built with the latest Android tools.
